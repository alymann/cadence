About the Comic

Cadence is Key is an interactive comic book about a young girl named Melody discovering her identity through music.

Melody doesn't struggle to fit in; she struggles to find herself. Though she has an amazing group of friends, she feels lost in the crowd. Her favorite band, First Kiss, seems to understand her in a way no one else does. They're everything she aspires to be: bold, creative, and unapologetically themselves.

When First Kiss announces a poster creating contest, she sees it as the perfect opportunity to prove to herself that she can be just as unique and talented as her idols. With her sketchbook in hand, Melody embarks on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and the power of music.
